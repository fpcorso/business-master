== Description ==

Business Master allows you to analyse your visitor information by capturing their information. Keep track of how your website is doing with Business Master.

= Features Include =
* Shows How Many Daily Visitors
* Shows How Many Daily Page Views
* Shows How Many Weekly Visitors
* Shows How Many Monthly Visitors
* Shows How Many Quarterly Visitors
* Compare Data To Previous Time Periods
* Shows What Countries Visitors Are From
* Shows What Browser Visitors Use
* Shows What Websites Referred Them To Your Website

**This plugin is in early beta. Please use the support forum or widget inside plugin to give feedback or suggestions.**
